# Prompt Engineering Kya Hai?

Prompt Engineering ek art hai jisme aap AI (jaise ChatGPT ya Claude) se sahi sawal poochtay hain taaki aapko best aur exact answer mil sake.

AI ka dimaag bahut tez hai, lekin usko direction dena zaroori hai. "Sahi sawal = Sahi jawab".

## Ache Prompt Likhne Ke Rules

Ek acha prompt banate waqt in 4 baaton ka dhyan rakhein:

1. **Context (Mahol):** AI ko batao ki situation kya hai. (e.g. "Main ek web developer hoon...")
2. **Task (Kaam):** AI ko clearly batao ki kya karna hai. (e.g. "Mujhe ek JavaScript function likh kar do...")
3. **Format (Tareeqa):** Jawab kis tarah chahiye? (e.g. "Table format me", "Bullet points me")
4. **Tone (Andaaz):** Jawab ka style kaisa hona chahiye? (e.g. "Simple bhasha me", "Professional tone me")

## Example of a Bad vs Good Prompt

### ❌ Bad Prompt (Bura Tareeqa)
> "Weight loss ke baare mein batao."
*(Ye bahut vague hai, AI lamba aur general answer dega).*

### ✅ Good Prompt (Acha Tareeqa)
> "Main ek 25 saal ka IT professional hoon jiska kaam din bhar baithne ka hai. Mujhe wazan kam karne ke liye ek simple 7-day diet plan aur exercise routine do. Jawab points mein dena aur tone friendly rakhna."
*(Ye specific hai aur AI ko poori jankaari de raha hai).*

## Top Prompt Frameworks

- **R.T.F. (Role, Task, Format):** "Ek fitness coach ban jao (Role), ek diet plan banao (Task), table format mein (Format)."
- **Chain of Thought:** AI ko kaho "Step by step socho". Isse maths aur logic wale problems behtar solve hote hain.
